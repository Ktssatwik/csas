Customer Analytics
1) Top N customers by spending
   - Total spend per customer = SUM(quantity * price)
   - Return: customer_id, name, city, total_spend, total_orders
2) Top N frequent customers by number of orders
   - Count orders per customer
   - Return: customer_id, name, city, orders_count
3) Number of customers from each city
   - Count customers grouped by city
   - Return: city, customer_count
4) Customer lifetime value (Top N)
   - Total spend, avg order value, customer lifespan
   - Return: customer_id, name, city, total_orders, total_spend, avg_order_value, customer_lifespan_days
5) Repeat vs new customers
   - Repeat = customers with >1 order
   - Return: customer_type (Repeat/New), customer_count
6) Customer order frequency
   - Distribution of number of orders per customer
   - Return: order_count, num_customers

Product Analysis
1) Top N products by revenue
   - Revenue per product = SUM(quantity * price)
   - Return: product_id, product_name, category, total_revenue
2) Top N products by items sold
   - Quantity per product = SUM(quantity)
   - Return: product_id, product_name, category, total_quantity
3) Number of items sold per category
   - Quantity per category = SUM(quantity)
   - Return: category, total_quantity
4) Revenue by category
   - Revenue per category = SUM(quantity * price)
   - Return: category, total_revenue
5) Underperforming products (lowest revenue)
   - Define as lowest total revenue with at least 1 sale
   - Return: product_id, product_name, category, total_revenue
   - Use LIMIT N and ORDER BY total_revenue ASC
6) Product price vs revenue
   - Compare product price to revenue and units sold
   - Return: product_name, category, price, total_units_sold, total_revenue
7) Average price by category
   - Return: category, avg_price, product_count

Sales Analysis
1) Monthly sales trend (by order date)
   - Revenue per month = SUM(quantity * price)
   - Return: month (YYYY-MM), total_revenue
2) Number of orders across each month
   - Count orders per month
   - Return: month (YYYY-MM), orders_count
3) Revenue by each city
   - Revenue per city = SUM(quantity * price)
   - Return: city, total_revenue
4) Order status breakdown
   - Count orders by status
   - Return: status, orders_count, percentage_of_total
5) Month-over-month revenue growth
   - Growth % compared to previous month
   - Return: month, total_revenue, prev_revenue, growth_pct
6) Revenue by weekday
   - Revenue and orders by day of week
   - Return: weekday, day_num, total_revenue, orders_count
7) City x category revenue heatmap
   - Revenue per city/category pair
   - Return: city, category, total_revenue

Order Analysis
1) Average order value
   - Total revenue / number of orders
   - Return: avg_order_value
2) Average items per order
   - Total quantity / number of orders
   - Return: avg_items_per_order
3) Order value distribution
   - Bucket orders into spend tiers
   - Return: value_bucket, orders_count
4) High value orders (Top N)
   - Highest total order values
   - Return: order_id, customer_name, city, order_date, status, order_total, distinct_products
